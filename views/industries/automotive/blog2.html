The Transformative Impact of IoT on the Automotive Industry with Boodskap.io

  • Predictive maintenance is one of the most significant applications of IoT in the automotive industry. Boodskap.io integrates various IoT sensors such as vibration sensors, temperature sensors, and pressure sensors to monitor vehicle health in real-time.   • Extended Vehicle Lifespan: Proactive maintenance extends the lifespan of vehicles by addressing issues before they cause significant damage.

  • Enhanced Safety: Real-time monitoring of driving behavior and vehicle health enhances driver and vehicle safety.
