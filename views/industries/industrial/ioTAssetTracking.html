ASSET TRACKING

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
  • Boodskap's IoT platform revolutionizes asset tracking in industrial environments by providing real-time visibility and control over valuable assets. Using a combination of IoT sensors and advanced tracking technologies, Boodskap helps businesses efficiently manage and optimize their assets, reducing losses and enhancing operational efficiency.
    • How Boodskap is Used
    1.Asset Tagging
  • Boodskap integrates IoT sensors such as RFID tags, GPS sensors, and proximity sensors to tag and monitor assets.
  • These sensors are attached to machinery, tools, inventory, and other critical assets to continuously track their location and status.
    • 2.Data Transmission
  • The tagged asset data is transmitted to the Boodskap IoT platform using reliable IoT protocols like LoRaWAN, Zigbee, and NB-IoT.
  • This ensures secure and efficient data transmission, allowing for seamless integration with existing systems.
    • 3.Real-Time Tracking
  • Boodskap's platform provides real-time tracking of assets, displaying their current location and movement on customizable dashboards.
  • This real-time visibility helps in locating assets quickly and accurately, reducing the time spent on manual searches.
    • 4.Geofencing and Alerts
  • The platform enables the creation of geofences around specific areas. When an asset moves outside these predefined boundaries, alerts are triggered.
  • Instant notifications are sent to relevant personnel, ensuring prompt action can be taken to prevent theft or loss.
    • 5.Data Analytics and Reporting
  • Boodskap's platform analyzes the tracking data to provide insights into asset utilization and movement patterns.
  • Customizable reports and analytics help in optimizing asset deployment and improving operational efficiency.
    • 6.Integration and Scalability
  • Boodskap's platform integrates with existing ERP and inventory management systems, providing a unified view of asset data.
  • It is scalable, allowing businesses to track an increasing number of assets as they grow.
    • Benefits
    Improved Asset Utilization
  • Optimize the use of assets by tracking their location and status in real-time.
    • Reduced Losses and Theft
  • Minimize asset loss and theft through real-time monitoring and geofencing alerts.
    • Enhanced Operational Efficiency
  • Save time and resources by reducing manual searches for assets.
    • Better Decision Making
  • Gain valuable insights into asset utilization and movement patterns for informed decision-making.
