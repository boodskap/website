Supply Chain Visibility with Boodskap's IoT Platform

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
  • Supply chain visibility using IoT technology provides end-to-end tracking and monitoring of goods throughout the supply chain. By leveraging IoT-enabled devices and sensors, companies can achieve real-time visibility into the location, condition, and movement of products. This advanced approach minimizes delays, reduces losses, and enhances coordination with suppliers and logistics partners. Boodskap's IoT Platform as a Service (PaaS) offers comprehensive solutions for supply chain visibility, integrating various sensors and devices to deliver actionable insights and predictive analytics.
    • How Boodskap is Used
  • Boodskap’s IoT platform enables businesses to implement supply chain visibility solutions that use data from various sensors to monitor the movement and condition of goods continuously. The platform’s robust data analytics and integration capabilities provide real-time tracking, condition monitoring, and detailed supply chain reports, ensuring optimal management and transparency across the entire supply chain.
    • Devices and Sensors Used Devices:
  • GPS trackers for real-time location tracking
  • RFID readers for automated identification
  • Mobile devices for data collection and monitoring
    • Sensors:
  • GPS Sensors: Track the real-time location of goods during transit.
  • RFID Tags: Automate the identification and tracking of items.
  • Temperature Sensors: Monitor the storage conditions of temperature-sensitive goods.
  • Humidity Sensors: Ensure optimal storage conditions by monitoring humidity levels.
  • Vibration Sensors: Detect any unusual handling or damage during transit.
    • IoT Protocols Used
  • GPS: Provides precise location tracking of goods.
  • RFID: Facilitates the automated identification and tracking of items.
  • LoRaWAN: Provides long-range connectivity for sensors across the supply chain.
  • MQTT (Message Queuing Telemetry Transport): Ensures real-time data transmission between supply chain sensors and the IoT platform.
    • Implementation Example
  • A logistics company using Boodskap’s IoT platform equips its trucks with GPS trackers and installs RFID readers at key points in the supply chain. Each shipment is tagged with an RFID tag that communicates with the RFID readers. Temperature and humidity sensors are placed inside shipping containers to monitor storage conditions. Vibration sensors detect any unusual handling during transit. All collected data is transmitted via LoRaWAN to the Boodskap platform. The platform analyzes the data, providing real-time insights into the location, condition, and movement of goods. Logistics managers can view detailed supply chain reports on a dashboard, enabling them to make timely decisions to optimize the supply chain and address any issues promptly.
    • Benefits
  • Real-Time Tracking: Provides continuous visibility into the location and movement of goods.
  • Condition Monitoring: Ensures optimal storage conditions for temperature-sensitive and perishable goods.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamlines supply chain processes, reducing delays and improving coordination with suppliers and logistics partners.
  • Cost Savings: Minimizes losses and damage, leading to cost savings in the supply chain.
    • By leveraging Boodskap’s IoT platform, businesses can implement advanced supply chain visibility solutions that enhance tracking, improve operational efficiency, and provide real-time insights into the supply chain, leading to better decision-making and optimized supply chain management.
