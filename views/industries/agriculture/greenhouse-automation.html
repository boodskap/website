    Devices
  • Climate control systems for regulating temperature and humidity

    • Sensors:
  • Temperature Sensors: Monitor the ambient temperature inside the greenhouse.
  • Humidity Sensors: Measure the moisture levels in the air.
  • CO2 Sensors: Track carbon dioxide levels to ensure adequate air quality.
  • Light Sensors: Monitor the intensity and duration of light exposure.
  • Soil Moisture Sensors: Measure the moisture content in the soil.
    • IoT Protocols Used
  • Zigbee: Suitable for creating a mesh network of sensors and control systems within the greenhouse.
  • Wi-Fi: Provides robust connectivity for data transmission within the greenhouse.
  • LoRaWAN: Used for long-range communication between greenhouse sensors and the central IoT platform.
  • MQTT: A lightweight messaging protocol for real-time data communication between sensors and the IoT platform.
